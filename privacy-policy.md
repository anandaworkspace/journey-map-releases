# Privacy Policy for Journey Map

Effective date: To be finalized before publication.

Journey Map is a personal mindmap and task-tracking app. The app is designed to keep your goals, notes, task lists, card layout, and images on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

## Information Stored by the App

Journey Map may store the following information locally on your device:

- Mindmap cards, notes, goals, and tasks
- Card positions, colors, sizes, and layout settings
- Images you add to the ME or Ultimate Life Goals cards
- App preferences such as zoom and saved layout state

## Information Sent to Us

The current version does not require an account and does not intentionally send your mindmap content to us.

## Advertising

Future versions may include Google AdMob advertising. If ads are added, Google and its partners may collect advertising identifiers, approximate device information, and usage signals according to Google's advertising policies. This policy should be updated before ads are enabled in production.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal mindmap content. If you export, back up, or share your map, that action is controlled by you.

## Children

Journey Map is intended for general productivity and personal planning. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Contact

Contact details should be added before publishing on Google Play.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app adds features such as cloud sync, analytics, or advertising.
